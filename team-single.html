About me

With over 27 years of experience in the pharmaceutical industry, Mr. Siddharth Singh has dedicated his career to advancing healthcare and building lasting relationships with healthcare professionals across India. Throughout his journey, he has worked closely with leading doctors, hospitals, and healthcare institutions, gaining deep insights into patient needs and evolving therapeutic challenges. His extensive industry experience, combined with a strong commitment to ethical business practices and quality healthcare, laid the foundation for the creation of Siidvan Healthcare. Driven by a vision to make a meaningful difference in patient lives, Mr. Singh established Siidvan Healthcare as a specialty-focused pharmaceutical company committed to delivering innovative, reliable, and accessible healthcare solutions. Under his leadership, the company continues to focus on excellence, integrity, and long-term value creation for patients, healthcare professionals, and society.
