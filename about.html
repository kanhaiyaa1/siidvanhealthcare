Founder's Intro

Mr. Siddharth Singh

Founder & Managing Director

With over 27 years of experience in the pharmaceutical industry, Mr. Siddharth Singh has dedicated his career to advancing healthcare and building lasting relationships with healthcare professionals across India.

Throughout his journey, he has worked closely with leading doctors, hospitals, and healthcare institutions, gaining deep insights into patient needs and evolving clinical challenges. His extensive industry experience, combined with a strong commitment to ethical business practices and quality healthcare, laid the foundation for the creation of Siidvan Healthcare.

Driven by a vision to make a meaningful difference in patient lives, Mr. Singh established Siidvan Healthcare as a specialty-focused pharmaceutical company committed to delivering innovative, reliable, and accessible healthcare solutions. Under his leadership, the company continues to focus on excellence, integrity, and long-term value creation for patients, healthcare professionals, and society.

A Message from Our Founder

Welcome to Siidvan Healthcare.

For more than two decades, I have had the privilege of working alongside some of the finest healthcare professionals in the country. This journey has taught me that behind every prescription lies a patient seeking hope, healing, and a better quality of life.

Siidvan Healthcare was founded with a simple yet powerful purpose—to make trusted, quality healthcare solutions accessible to those who need them most. Our commitment extends beyond medicines; it is about building trust, supporting medical professionals, and contributing to healthier communities.

As we continue to grow, our focus remains unwavering: to uphold the highest standards of quality, integrity, and patient care while delivering meaningful innovations that improve lives.

Mr. Siddharth Singh
